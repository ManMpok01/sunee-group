About Us

We are a leading freight logistics company based in Lubumbashi, D.R. Congo, with a branch in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. We provide reliable local and international shipping services for businesses and individuals, ensuring shipment security and innovative solutions. Trust our expertise for a seamless logistics experience.

what we do

Logistics and Transportation

We provide end-to-end logistics and transportation services, both locally and internationally. Our solutions are designed to optimize the supply chain, ensuring timely and cost-effective delivery of goods. We offer a range of services, including warehousing, distribution, freight forwarding, and last-mile delivery. Our advanced logistics technology and experienced team ensure that your products are handled with care and delivered on time, every time.

more

Mining Industry

We offer specialized logistics services tailored to the mining sector, including the supply of essential mining products and the sale and transportation of heavy mining equipment. Our team is experienced in managing the complex logistics requirements of mining operations, from the transportation of raw materials to the delivery of large-scale machinery. We ensure the seamless movement of materials and equipment, supporting the efficiency and safety of your mining projects.

More

Agriculture

Our logistics solutions cater to the agricultural sector, providing the supply of agricultural products and the sale and transportation of agricultural machinery. We support the agricultural community by facilitating the efficient movement of goods and equipment, ensuring that farmers and agribusinesses have access to the resources they need. Our services include the transportation of seeds, fertilizers, and machinery, as well as the management of supply chains to optimize productivity and reduce costs.

more

International Trade

We offer comprehensive customs clearance services and international transportation solutions, helping businesses navigate the complexities of global commerce. Our expertise in international trade ensures smooth and efficient cross-border transactions, from documentation and compliance to the transportation of goods. We provide end-to-end support, including import/export services, freight forwarding, and real-time tracking, ensuring that your products reach their destination efficiently and compliantly.

More

Agro-Food Industry

Our company specializes in the supply and transportation of food products, ensuring the safe and efficient distribution of both perishable and non-perishable goods. We maintain the highest standards of quality and freshness, leveraging advanced logistics solutions to meet the unique needs of the agro-food sector. Our services include temperature-controlled storage, real-time tracking, and timely delivery, ensuring that your products reach their destination in perfect condition.
